The Tinkerer's Legacy
  • Mission 1: A Fragment of Genius
    Can be found on the 3rd floor in the Toy Factory, big building East/North East of Junction with a Big Brown Toy Bear on top of the building, approx. 450m East of Junction
  • Mission 2: The Spinning Crystal
    Can be found on the 2nd floor in the Parkside Mall Jeweller's Store, South West of Junction
  • Mission 3: Threads of Harmony
    Can be found on the ground floor inside the 'Special Repairs' Factory Building approx. 350m East of Junction (Near a Raider base)
  • Mission 4: Box of Secrets
    Can be found inside the closest building at a Junkyard 1.5 km South/South East of Junction
  • Mission 5: The Artisan's Key
    Can be found at the Lumbermill South/South East of Junction, near where you get the Yellow Summer Dress for Miles Miller, approx 600m East/South East of Junction
