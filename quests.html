Memento: Lost Heirloom
  • Follow the 'Bones' Trail leading away from the grave (Mr. Snoop is located at level -1 in the underground garage around 100m West from the Grave)
Electrifying The Stronghold: Calibrating The Current

The correct order for the switches:

  • Gym (Level 0)
  • Construction Site (East Junction, Level +2)
  • Diner (Middle Junction, Level +1)
  • Community Center (Big building with Auction House, Level 0)
  • Violet's Flower Shop (Level 0)
The Tinkerer's Legacy
  • Mission 1: Can be found on the 3rd floor in the Toy Factory, big building East/North East of Junction with a Big Brown Toy Bear on top of the building, approx. 450m East of Junction
  • Mission 2: The Spinning Crystal - Can be found on the 2nd floor in the Parkside Mall Jeweller's Store, South West of Junction
  • Mission 3: Threads of Harmony - Can be found on the ground floor inside the 'Special Repairs' Factory Building approx. 350m East of Junction (Near a Raider base)
  • Mission 4: Box of Secrets - Can be found inside the closest building at a Junkyard 1.5 km South/South East of Junction
  • Mission 5: The Artisan's Key - Can be found at the Lumbermill South/South East of Junction, near where you get the Yellow Summer Dress for Miles Miller, approx 600m East/South East of Junction
