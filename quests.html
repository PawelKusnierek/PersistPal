This guide tells you when quests become available, so you can pick them up as soon as possible.

I recommend getting level 4 while doing the initial 'Humble Beginnings' quest for Eli Dawson, then when you get back, you can pick up the first two sets of quests.

After you choose your weapon reward from Marcia Ramirez:

  • Marcia Ramirez
  • Eli Dawson (Gym)
  • Stanley McDowell (Diner)
  • Dean Alfred (East area of Junction)
  • Derrick Walsh (East area of Junction)

Level 4:
  • Marcia Ramirez
  • Violet Hartwell (Flower Shop)
  • Charlie Hobson (Gas Station)
  • Eli Dawson (Gym)
  • Derrick Walsh (East area of Junction)

Level 10:
  • Phil Wagner (Library)
  • Violet Hartwell (Flower Shop)
  • Charlie Hobson (Gas Station)
  • Nina Brooks (Gas Station)
  • Henry R. Cass (Wanders around Junction, Traffic Cone Hat)
