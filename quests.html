This section tells you when & where certain quests become available, so that you can pick them up as soon as possible.

After initial 'Tutorial' Quests with Marcia (after you choose your first weapon rewards):
I recommend going for level 4 while doing the initial 'Humble Beginnings' quest for Eli Dawson, then you can pick up everything below + all level 4 quests.

  • Marcia Ramirez
  • Eli Dawson (Gym)
  • Stanley McDowell (Diner)
  • Dean Alfred (Fisherman, East area of Junction)
  • Derrick Walsh (East area of Junction)


Level 4:
  • Violet Hartwell (Flower Shop)
  • Charlie Hobson (Gas Station)
  • Marcia Ramirez
  • Eli Dawson (Gym)
  • Derrick Walsh (East area of Junction)

Level 10:
  • Phil Wagner (Library)
  • Violet Hartwell (Flower Shop)
  • Charlie Hobson (Gas Station)
  • Nina Brooks (Gas Station)
  • Henry R. Cass (Wanders around Junction, Traffic Cone Hat)
